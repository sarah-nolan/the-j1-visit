Paying U.S. Taxes

If you received a paycheck or stipen form your host organization during your J1 visit, then you may have to file a U.S. income tax return.

When?

If you paid at any point in 2017, then you will need to submit your tax return by April 15, 2018.

How?

You will likely be able to file a 1040-EX online. Please visit the website of the U.S. Internal Revenue Service for more details.
