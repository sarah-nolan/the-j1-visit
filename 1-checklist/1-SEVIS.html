Activate your J1 Status

Please submit the form below to activate your J1 visa status. You should do this on the first or second day of your program.

If you submit this form later than 30 days from the program start date listed on your DS-2019, then your J1 visa status will become invalid. Please contact IA&A if you foresee a delay.
