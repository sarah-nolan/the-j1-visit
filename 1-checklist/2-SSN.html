Social Security Number

As soon as your J1 visa status has been validated by your sponsor in SEVIS, you may apply for a U.S. Social Security Number (SSN).

You should follow the Social Security Administration's instructions for "Original Card, Adult, Non-resident".

Your host will need your SSN in order to process any stipend, honorarium, salary, per diem, or any other compensation provided to you.
