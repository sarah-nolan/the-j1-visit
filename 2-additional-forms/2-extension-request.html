Extension Request

J1 intern programs may be up to 12 months long and J1 trainee programs may be up to 18 months long. If your current program duration is currently less than the maximum, then you may be eligible for an extension.

Please submit your J1 extension request at least 2 weeks before your current program is scheduled to end so that your sponsor can evaluate and complete the necessary paperwork in time. If you are requesting an extension with a new host, please allow a 4 week processing time. See change of host request instructions for more details.
