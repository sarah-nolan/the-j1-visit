Travel Validation Request

If you wish to travel outside the U.S. at any point during your J1 program, then you will need a travel validation signature from your sponsor on your DS-2019.

Your sponsor must receive your travel validation request a minimum of 2 weeks before the date that you plan to leave the U.S. Your sponsor must confirm your active status with your host and return the DS-2019 signed to you before your departure.
